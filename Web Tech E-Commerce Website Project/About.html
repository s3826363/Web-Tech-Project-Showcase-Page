Our Story

Sakura Teriyaki Grill is a neighborhood Japanese restaurant in Jacksonville, Florida, inspired by quick-service spots like Panera and local favorites, but focused on teriyaki.

Our goal is to serve fresh, affordable meals that fit into busy schedules—whether you’re a student, a family on the go, or a professional grabbing lunch between meetings.

Our Values

  • Fresh Ingredients – Prepared throughout the day for quality flavor.
  • Fast & Friendly – Order online or in person with a simple, clear menu.
  • Local Community – Proud to be part of Jacksonville’s food scene.
